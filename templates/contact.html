Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All image processing is done locally in your browser. Your images are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file formats do you support?

We support all major image formats including JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, BMP, and TIFF. Each tool has specific format requirements.

Is there a file size limit?

We recommend files under 50MB for optimal performance. Larger files may take longer to process but are still supported.

Are your tools really free?

Yes! All our tools are completely free to use with no hidden costs, watermarks, or limitations. We believe in making powerful tools accessible to everyone.
